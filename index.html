Mbappé scores, but France only draws with Poland and advances as 2nd in the group

From UOL, in São Paulo
25/06/2024 14h55

Upon Mbappé’s return, France only managed a 1–1 draw with Poland and let the lead of Group D in the Euro slip away. The result this Tuesday (25), combined with Austria’s victory over the Netherlands, pushed the French down to second place.

France advanced to the Round of 16, while Poland said goodbye to the Euro. The Poles finished at the bottom of the group with just one point. Austria topped Group D with six points.

Goalkeeper Skorupski held off Mbappé in the first half, but the number 10 came out on top with a penalty. The keeper was Poland’s standout player in the match and saved three of the French number 10’s shots during open play, but the striker prevailed from the penalty spot.
